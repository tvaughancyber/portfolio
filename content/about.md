I'm Tyler — an aspiring offensive security professional focused on penetration testing, vulnerability research, and translating technical findings into clear writing.

## What I do

I spend most of my time on hands-on security work: testing web applications, working through CTF challenges, and digging into how systems break under pressure. I treat every engagement as a chance to sharpen the loop of *recon → exploit → document* — the last step matters as much as the first two.

## Why writeups

Anyone can run a tool. The harder skill is explaining *why* a vulnerability matters, *how* it was found, and *what* a defender should do about it — in language a non-technical stakeholder can act on. That's the muscle this site is built to train.
